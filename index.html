HISTORY of EASTWOODS College of Science and Technology
(formerly EASTWOODS International Institute of Science and Technology)


EASTWOODS International Institute of Science and Technology, was the 3rd institution founded in Bataan by the President Mr. Gregorio M. Sison Jr,
where the first was EASTWOODS Professional College of Science and Technology (formerly SOFTNET College) in Balanga City founded in 1994, and
second was SOFTNET Information Technology Center in Mariveles founded in 1997.

During the first year of operation, it already hit 196 students renting the 3rd and 2nd Floor of the 3-storey Masagana Bldg. On 2007, its second year of
operation, EASTWOODS - Dinalupihan increased the number of enrollees to 434 students and already occupied the whole building.
